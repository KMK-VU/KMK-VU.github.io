Teaching ... during lockdown

MOOC platforms like Coursera have been available for a while. Their main purpose is to reach a wide audience by getting rid off the physical limits of traditional teaching. Let's emulate such platforms to adress the issue of teaching during lockdown.

This project will actually be used in real condition as part of machine learning courses I will give at Université Paris Dauphine.

The university currently uses Teams as default tool to communicate with students. Live communication with students as well as class recordings will therefore be made through Teams.

This online teaching platform will focus on 2 main tasks: delivering courses materials, implementing online homeworks and exam.

Specifications:
  • Course materials: Being able to display theoric demonstrations as well as practical programming interactions
  • Homeworks and exams: Interactive solution to display homeworks/exams and store students handovers and results.
IT stack used:
  • UI: HTML/CSS/JS
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQL
  • Contents: Javascript / Reveal.js (slides)
