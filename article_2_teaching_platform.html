Teaching ... while in lockdown

I will be giving a course in data mining / machine learning with Samira Bouadi at the beginning of next year for Universit√© Paris Dauphine master. However, since all universities are currently closed in France due to the lockdown, we have to come up with solutions for teaching exclusively online.

As MOOC platforms already tackle this issue, I wanted to create my own mini-platform where students could log-in and access all materials including homeworks.

Specifications:
  • Course materials: Display slides
  • Homeworks + Exams: Display quizz/exams and store students results

The university uses Teams as default tool to communicate with students. Live communication with students as well as class recordings will therefore be made through Teams.

IT stacks:
  • UI: HTML/CSS/JS
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQL
  • Contents: Javascript / Reveal.js (slides)
