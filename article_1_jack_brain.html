Let's start the cooking experimentation series with a funny challenge: creating a meal that fits with the Halloween theme !

For the decoration, a halloween pumpkin is the first thing that comes to mind. But using a pumpkin feels like cheating. So let's take oranges instead and use them as receptacle!

Then the meal. Something original but still tasty and that also fits halloween theme. Blood ? Gaspacho ! Having tomatoes and orange pulp from the decoration, why not try a gaspacho that combined both ? Luckily there is a simple receipt that fits the bill here.

In the end, the carving of the oranges was the most challenging part as everything else was simply done with the mixer.

And here is the result:

While the main focus was on the decoration, the meal in itself tasted really nice !

Quick notes: Need to chop a bit the bottom of the oranges so that they stand without falling but not too much to keep the gaspacho from leaking. For a nicer texture (it was meant to look like blood), maybe peel all vegetables and add beet juice for the colour.
