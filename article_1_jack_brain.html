Let's start the cooking experimentation series with a funny challenge: creating a meal that fits with the Halloween theme !

For the decoration, I looked at previous deserts done in the past and aranged the presentation for the theme by carving it like a halloween pumpkin.

Regarding the meal, I wanted something liquid and red to make it like blood. Having tomatoes and orange pulp from the decoration, I then decided to make a gaspacho that combined both. Luckily, a simple receipt was already available here.

Here is the final result !

Quick notes: Need to chope a bit the bottom of the oranges so that they stand without falling but not too much to keep the gaspacho from leaking. For a nicer texture (it was meant to look like blood...), maybe peel all vegetables and add beet juice for the colour.
